The IVY Difference

Ivy International Group provides unparalleled level of year-round support to our students, their families, and our partner schools. It is our mission to keep students at the center of all that we do as they navigate through American culture and its distinctive high school experience.

Our Mission

To provide a top-level experience for our international students at American high schools while supporting our partner schools by creating a worldly synergy and multicultural experience.
